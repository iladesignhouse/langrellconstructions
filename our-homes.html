- Our Homes -

All of our custom homes are unique and reflect our clients. We take the time to get to know our clients, understand the way they live and design homes that meet their individual needs.

65 Canopy's Edge Blvd

Custom designed new residence, this home was designed to offer style and privacy with a single level open-plan layout with the huge internal living area opening onto the outdoor entertainment patio, gardens and inground pool.

The large master bedroom is positioned privately from the other bedrooms and includes a modern ensuite and walk-in-robe. The other three bedrooms all feature mirrored built in cupboards, plush carpet and air conditioning throughout.

The main bathroom is designed with a walk-in shower, floor to ceiling tiles and a gorgeous free standing bath - the perfect place to relax and unwind.


26 Amberwood Cres

Custom designed two level residence in the exclusive Canopy's Edge Estate. Built with a blend of modern and sophisticated design using neutral tones and textures throughout, this home is ideal for relaxed, laid back living in the tropics.

The large modern master bedroom and ensuite are privately positioned to look out over the rainforest. The other three bedrooms all feature mirrored built in cupboards, plush carpet and air conditioning.

This house was designed with an open plan layout to create a spacious light filled feel throughout. It features a large contemporary kitchen and outdoor patio overlooking the forest - the perfect house to entertain.
